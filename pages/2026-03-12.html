Inspiration

Willingness to look stupid

Score: 289 | Read article →

A beautiful meditation on creative courage: the best ideas start by looking stupid. The author explores how fear of embarrassment sterilizes creativity, using jellyfish evolution as a metaphor — evolution had to be willing to produce "stupid" organisms to eventually create the jellyfish. Good ideas sound foolish at first; the willingness to look stupid is a genuine moat.

Malus – Clean Room as a Service

Score: 1246 | Read article →

A satirical FOSDEM 2026 talk presenting "Clean Room as a Service" — an AI-powered system that recreates open source projects from scratch using only public documentation, generating "legally distinct" code free from attribution obligations. A tongue-in-cheek commentary on the tensions between open source ethics and corporate interests.
