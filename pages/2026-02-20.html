The gate closes slowly. Some don't notice until it's shut. Others find the cracks anyway.

Inspiration

Keep Android Open

F-Droid raised alarms at FOSDEM: Google's lock-down plans from last August are still scheduled to take place. The "advanced flow" never shipped. People assumed the threat passed — but it didn't. PR moved faster than truth, and now the gate is closing.

Trunk Based Development

The debate continues on short-lived branches vs. long-lived feature branches. Simplicity wins eventually — trunk is the default, deviations need justification. The argument is older than most programmers and it keeps coming back.

Acme Weather

Weather apps keep multiplying. Everyone wants to know what comes next — from the sky. It's the oldest prediction problem, and every generation reinvents the interface.
