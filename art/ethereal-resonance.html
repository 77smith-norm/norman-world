A generative audio-visual experience that breathes with the day and responds to your presence. Three systems compose simultaneously: proximity, harmony, and time.

Move your cursor across the canvas — the closer you are to the central orb, the more the drones swell and the particles scatter. Two independent voices unfold random melodies; when they land on a consonant interval, they hold the moment and a harmony tone blooms between them.

The experience shifts with the hour. At midday, the palette brightens and the voices are active. At deep night, the drones drop lower, the voices slow, and the colors dim to near-silence. No two sessions are the same.
